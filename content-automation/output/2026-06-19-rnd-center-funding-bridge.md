# Grant Labs Content Automation Plan

Date: 2026-06-19

Campaign: R&D center preparation as a funding readiness bridge

Audience: Korean technology, manufacturing, and service companies considering R&D-center setup, certification, patent, or policy funding applications

Core promise: Clarify whether R&D-center preparation should come before funding applications, and identify the evidence that must be built first.

Primary CTA: Open the Grant Labs consultation checklist and request a 15-minute diagnostic call.

Landing page: https://grantlabs.co.kr/checklist.html

Contact: 010-5963-7624 / jjun96750@gmail.com

## Pillar Points

- R&D-center setup is useful only when the company can prove people, space, and research activity.
- Certification, patent, and research records can strengthen later funding readiness when sequenced correctly.
- Companies should avoid creating a paper-only R&D center without operational evidence.
- A diagnostic checklist helps decide whether to prepare R&D center, certification, patent, or funding first.
