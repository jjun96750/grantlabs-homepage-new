Core promise: Before applying, identify the missing evidence, eligibility gaps, and next action order.

Primary CTA: Open the Grant Labs consultation checklist and request a 15-minute diagnostic call.

Landing page: https://grantlabs.co.kr/checklist.html

## Pillar Points

- Recent sales, tax, employment, and debt data decide the first diagnostic path.
- Certification and R&D-center preparation can improve funding readiness when timed correctly.
- Application order matters because some evidence must exist before the funding window opens.
- A short readiness checklist prevents wasted applications and rushed document work.
