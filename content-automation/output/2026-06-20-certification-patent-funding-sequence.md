# Grant Labs Content Automation Plan

Date: 2026-06-20

Campaign: Certification, patent, and policy funding preparation sequence

Audience: Korean founders and SMEs deciding whether to prepare certification, patent, R&D center evidence, or policy funding first

Core promise: Help founders decide which proof to prepare first so certification, patent, and funding work support each other instead of moving separately.

Primary CTA: Open the Grant Labs consultation checklist and request a 15-minute diagnostic call.

Landing page: https://grantlabs.co.kr/checklist.html

## Pillar Points

- Certification is stronger when it is backed by real operating records, not just an application form.
- Patent preparation should connect to the company's product, research activity, and commercialization plan.
- Policy funding readiness improves when certification, patent, and financial evidence are sequenced before the announcement rush.
- A short diagnostic can prevent wasted work by deciding whether certification, patent, R&D center, or funding should come first.
