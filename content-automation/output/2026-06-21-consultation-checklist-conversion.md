# Grant Labs Content Automation Plan

Date: 2026-06-21

Campaign: Consultation checklist to diagnostic call conversion

Audience: Korean founders and SME operators who are unsure what to prepare before asking about policy funding, certification, R&D center, patent, or incorporation support

Core promise: Turn vague interest in funding or certification into a prepared 15-minute diagnostic conversation with the right documents and questions.

Primary CTA: Open the Grant Labs consultation checklist, mark what you already have, and request a diagnostic call.

Landing page: https://grantlabs.co.kr/checklist.html

Contact: 010-5963-7624 / jjun96750@gmail.com

## Pillar Points

- Most consultation delays start because the founder does not know which documents matter first.
- A checklist reduces back-and-forth by separating business registration, revenue, employment, finance, certification, patent, and R&D evidence.
- The first call should identify eligibility signals and missing evidence, not promise funding approval.
- Prepared founders can decide faster whether policy funding, certification, R&D center, patent, or incorporation support should be reviewed first.
